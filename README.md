# Diatomaceous Earth Water Filter

## Overview
This project explores the use of diatomaceous earth as a filtration medium for removal of microplastics and water purification. The goal is to investigate whether a low-cost, accessible filtration system can improve water quality by reducing suspended particles and other contaminants.

## Motivation
Access to clean water remains a challenge in many regions. This project was developed to explore affordable and scalable filtration solutions using naturally occurring materials.

## Objectives
- Study the filtration properties of diatomaceous earth
- Design a prototype water filter
- Evaluate water clarity before and after filtration
- Investigate potential applications in low-resource settings

## Methodology
1. Construct a prototype filtration unit.
2. Incorporate diatomaceous earth as a filtration layer.
3. Pass water samples through the filter.
4. Record observations and performance metrics.
